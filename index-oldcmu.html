I am an Assistant Professor at CMU ECE working on wireless networks and mobile systems. I design and build systems that leverage a deep understanding of the wireless physical layer to enable faster wireless networks and deliver new services. My work has been featured as research highlights in the Communications of the ACM (CACM) and the International Journal of Robotics Research (IJRR). I am a recipient of the George Sprowls Award for best Ph.D thesis in Computer Science at MIT and the President of India gold medal at IIT Madras.
