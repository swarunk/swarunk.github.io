Project PushID: Pushing the Range Limits of Commercial Passive RFIDs

Today's commercial passive RFIDs report ranges of 5-10 meters at best. This reveals blindspots where RFIDs remain undetected in warehouses, stores and factories today. We present PushID which achieves an 8x improvement in range over commercial RFID systems.

Citation
